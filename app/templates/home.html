About JO-EN

We are a comprehensive enterprise specializing in global cross-border e-commerce and localized market execution. With TikTok Shop operations, TAP ecosystem capabilities, and Australian cross-border commerce as our core strengths, we empower Asia-Pacific brands with one-stop global expansion solutions.

JO-EN has established operational entities in Australia, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, and Malaysia, supported by mature local supply chains, experienced operating teams, and strong channel resources.

In Australia, we work closely with TikTok to build an innovative model that combines talent incubation with online-offline integration. Through livestream incubation, stage performance, and creator development, we create a complete pipeline from discovering raw talent to building professional entertainment assets.

Our business covers beauty, lifestyle products, automotive products, food, and apparel across multiple e-commerce categories. From brand onboarding and full-chain operations to local fulfillment and market expansion, we provide end-to-end solutions that help brands scale efficiently and sustainably.
